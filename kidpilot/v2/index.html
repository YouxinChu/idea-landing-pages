KidPilot is designed for kids ages 6-14. The AI buddy adapts its language and complexity based on your child's age and reading level.

Will my kid actually use it? +

That's the whole point. Unlike monitoring apps (which kids hate), KidPilot is something kids choose to open. The buddy remembers them, jokes with them, and grows with them. It's more like a Tamagotchi than a to-do list.
